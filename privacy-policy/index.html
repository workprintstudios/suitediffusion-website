Prompts, queries, and AI-generated outputs may be temporarily logged for service operation, model improvement, debugging, and session continuity.

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary to provide the Service or comply with legal obligations. AI prompts and outputs are stored temporarily and anonymized when used for model improvement. Usage logs are retained for a maximum of 12 months unless required by law.
